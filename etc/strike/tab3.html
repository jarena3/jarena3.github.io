Class

Apart from the Role, each character has a Class. These are the mechanics for actual combat, and where the majority of your combat abilities will come from. You should pick what seems fun and fits your character (see the reskinning note in the intro). Don't worry about the names of the classes as much as the description.

Necromancer
Command defeated enemies, revitalize fallen friends, and strike fear into the hearts of opponents.
Duellist
Pick a single target, isolate and destroy it, then repeat.
Archer
Total mastery of ranged attacks, covering fire, and suppressing fire.
Martial Artist
Switch between multiple fighting styles that have unique attack bonuses and passive effects.
Warlord
Command allies and give them additional movement, attacks, and buffs.
Magician
Use a variety of powerful, chaotic abilities.
Bombardier
Large area-of-effect attacks that deal damage to many enemies at once.

Necromancer

Class Feature: Mark of Death

The first time you hit a non-minion enemy with an attack, Mark it. When that enemy is taken out, gain the benefit of your Gift. Choose from one of the following three Gifts and add it to your character sheet as a Feat:

Reanimation When your Marked target is taken out, it stands up on its next Initiative count under your control. It has 1 hit point and collapses after its turn.
Terror When your Marked target is taken out, every creature within 3 squares takes 1 damage and makes a Saving Throw. If they fail, they are Distracted until the end of their next turn.
Vampirism When your Marked target is taken out, regenerate 1 HP. If you have full HP, your next attack does +1 damage. This effect continues to stack.

Duelist

Class Feature: Focus

When you use the Duel Encounter power gain 1 Focus. Each round that you remain focused on that target, grain an additional point of Focus. Focus can be spent on one of the following Feats (select only one):

Find an Opening When you attack your Duel target, spend any amount of Focus to increase damage by the amount spent.
Throw Off their Aim When the target of your Duel attacks an ally, spend any amount of Focus to reduce damage by the amount spent.
Force their Position When the target of your Duel ends its turn, spend any amount of Focus to slide your target one square per Focus spent.

Add the following Encounter power to your character sheet:

Encounter Duel 10⦾ Free Action
Target one creature within 10 squares. Until the end of the encounter, when you attack the target, any 2s you roll are treated as though they were 4s

At-Will Change Target 10⦾

Sentinel On your turn, as a Free Action, you may designate one character to overwatch. Deal 2 damage to the first enemy that enters a square adjacent to the designated character before your next turn. This counts as an opportunity attack.

Martial Artist

Class Feature: Stance Mastery

Begin each combat in one of your chosen Stances. Each Stance grants an active and passive ability, and Stance encounter powers are not expended when used. In addition, gain the following Feat and add it to your character sheet:

The False Death Instead of being taken out, reduce your total HP to 1. If you are hit again this encounter, you are taken out.

Name and add the following Encounter power to your character sheet:

Encounter Free Action
You drop all stances and lose the ability to take on stances for the remainder of the encounter, but in exchange you can take two Attack Actions and two Move Actions per round. If you are dazed, stunned, immobilized, or incapacitated, you lose this ability and regain Stances.

Name and add four of the following Encounter powers to your character sheet:

Encounter Stance

Encounter Stance
While you are in this stance, add the following effect to all your melee attacks: Slide target 2 squares.

While you are in this stance, your melee attacks have a range of 2.
Encounter Stance
While you are in this stance, add the following effect to all your melee attacks: Deal 2 damage to an enemy adjacent to the target.

While you are in this stance, enemies that end their turn adjacent to you take 1 damage.
Encounter Stance
While you are in this stance, add the following effect to all your melee attacks: Grab the target.

While you are in this stance, if an attack misses you while you have an enemy Grabbed, the Grabbed enemy takes 2 damage.
Encounter Stance

While you are in this stance, add 1 damage to your opportunity attacks.
Encounter Stance

Warlord

Class Feature: Support

When you hit with a power that deals "S", you take a Support Point but deal no damage. When you roll a 6 on such a power, take an additional Support Point. When you miss with any power, take a Support Token. Choose from one of the following three focuses and add it to your character sheet as a Feat:

Rousing Spend a Support Point when an ally starts their turn to give them 2 Temporary HP. You may not spend more than one per character on the same turn. Gain a Support Token whenever a teammate within 5 squares of you misses or fails a Saving Throw.
Enabling Spend a Support Point when an ally starts their turn to give them an extra three squares of movement. These squares are available regardless of how or if the ally uses their Move Action. You may not spend more than one per character on the same turn. Gain a Support Point whenever you give an ally Advantage on an attack by flanking the same target.
Incisive Spend a Support Point when an ally starts their turn to double the damage on their next attack roll if they hit. You may not spend more than one per character on the same turn. Gain a Support Point whenever you use the Assess power. You may spend a Move Action to use the Assess power.

Bombardier

Class Feature: Ammunition

Ammunition is used to empower your At-Will attacks. Select an ammunition specialization, add it as a Feat to your character sheet, and apply its effects every time you use an Ammo power in combat:

Shaped Charges You can utilize blasts of different shapes. When you select this specialization, pick two of the following:
  • Squares: a 3x3 square.
  • Rings: a ring including all tiles exactly 1 away from the center, or all tiles exactly 2 away from the center.
  • Crosses: a cross or an X extending 2 squares in four opposing directions from the central square.
  • Lines: a line extending in a single direction for 5 tiles.
Gelling Agent All zones created by Ammo-based attacks will last for one additional round.
