Hey, I couldn't think of a better way to generate character sheets for this new system. This page will step you through building a character, and hopefully give you everything you need to write down. Unless I fucked something up because I'm writing all of this in an endless series of meetings.

Characters in this game are made of two parts:

The first is Role, or the position you play in team combat. A sports analogy would be eg. linebacker or quarterback. What you do in relation to your teammates, and what your teammates look to you for.

The second is Class, which contains the mechanics behind your character's combat..

The central element of character creation is reskinning. This game gives you a bunch of options that probably don't really fit your character, but encourages you to change the narrative mechanisms of the option to fit your concept.
