Role

There's only one part that needs any real coordination between us, and that's role selection. Role is, like it sounds, the part your character plays in a battle. There are five:

Blaster
Long-range area attacks that deal damage and/or restrict what enemies can do on the battlefield. Blasters also gain the ability to deploy zones of dangerous or impassible terrain.
Controller
Locking down enemies and curtailing their ability to fight back. Controllers basic attacks can get stunning or sliding effects, and they also gain abilities to weaken, immobilize, restrain, silence, and blind enemies.
Defender
Absorbing damage and forcing enemy actions. Defenders gain bonus resistances and HP when they're damaged, and force enemies to attack them or incur massive damage and debility.
Leader
Boosting and healing allies. Leaders gain abilities that let their allies take extra movement, attacks, healing boosts, and the chance to re-roll dice.
Striker
Dealing massive damage to a single target. Strikers gain improved dodging, parrying, and counter strike abilities against a single target.

We should have at least one of each, but (probably) no more than two of a single role. I've made a list below, with suggestions for what characters might slot well into what role. Don't feel like this is what you need to play, it's just if you don't have any preference:
